How to make sushi

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely, but the one key ingredient is "sushi rice," also referred to as shari, or sumeshi.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Sumeshi

Steps

  1. Prep the Rice & Nori. Lay out the nori on the bamboo mat (or whatever you're using)
  2. Spread the Rice on the Nori.
  3. Add Fillings to the Bottom Quarter of the Nori.
  4. Roll the Sushi Part Way.
  5. Roll the Sushi the Rest of the Way.
  6. Move to Cutting Board.
  7. Cut the Sushi.
  8. Serve
