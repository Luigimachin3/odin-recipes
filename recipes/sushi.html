Sushi

Description

Try this fun and easy to make sushi rolls!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 sheet of nori
  • 1/4 cucumber, julienned
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2-3 imitation crab sticks or cooked shrimp

Steps

  1. Lay the nori sheet on a bamboo mat, shiny side down.
  2. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving 1 inch at the top edge.
  3. Arrange cucumber, avocado, and crab horizontally across the center.
  4. Roll tightly using the mat, sealing the edge with a little water.
  5. Slice into 6-8 pieces with a wet, sharp knife - serve with soy sauce.
